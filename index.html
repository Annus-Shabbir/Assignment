Small Industries In Pakistan:

Introduction:

Cottage and small scale industries are for labour and provide employment to the 80% of the people of rural areas.This work reduces rate of unimployment and offers opportunity for self employment.Traditionally,women are not given permission to work outside the homes so these small industries like: Candle making,Handicrafts e.t.c can be established in the homes.

Main body:

Cottage industries have gained very importance in cities and towns.There is a great demand of hand made things such as:Carpets,Cricket bats e.t.c.Hand made things are also have good quality that,s why people demand for hand made things.These goods can be exported to many others countries and profit can be gained.

Why is it important to support small industries:

Its very important to support small industries because many lights of homes are blown due to these small industries.People have adopted this talent as a profession now its their work,many people earn by small industries,many people run the lights of their homes by these industries so government and we all should promote and give importance to the small industries and more and more small industries should be established in rural areas so that,our enimployment rate lies to 0%.

Advantages of establishing small industries:

  1. Source of foreign exchange income.
  2. Provide employment to women.
  3. Reduces migration of people from rural to urban areas.
  4. Little burden on imports.
  5. Meets the demand of market.

These above are the basic advantages of small scale industries that,s why it should be established more and more.

Types of small industries in Pakistan:

Pakistani small industries includes:

  • Carpets

     Carpets play an important part in export.There are usualy hand wooven and made of silk and wool or mixture of two.

  • Textiles:

    • Textiles are found throughout the country with a variety of design and techniques.The most famous among are Kaddar,Khes,Shaal and Ajrak.

  • Sports Goods:

    • Sports goods earn 3.7% of our total export.Football,HockeyCricket bats and Rackets are mostly manufactured by hands.Sports goods are mainly made Sialkot.
